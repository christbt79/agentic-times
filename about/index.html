About

Why I Started This

Something is happening in artificial intelligence that most publications are not covering well. Not the chatbots, not the image generators — but the agents. The systems that don't just answer questions but take action, make decisions, and operate autonomously across hours and days without a human in the loop.

The Agentic Times exists because that shift deserves serious, clear-eyed coverage. Written for business leaders and technology enthusiasts who want to understand what agentic AI actually means — not the hype, not the fear, but the reality of what is being built and deployed right now.

This is a solo publication. One writer, one point of view, published a few times a week. If you have a story tip, a tool you think deserves coverage, or simply want to get in touch, I'd like to hear from you.
